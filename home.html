ENERGY DERIVATIVES BROKERS

Headquartered in Tinton Falls, New Jersey, Applegate Commodities LLC provides execution services for institutional customers with a primary focus on energy derivatives. Applegate is a boutique firm whose value comes from the experience, contacts and work ethic of its talented group of brokers. We leverage our global industry contacts, along with our ability to execute trades electronically, to provide customers with competitive fills, timely information, liquidity and anonymity.

OUR PRODUCTS
Applegate Commodities LLC offers clients the choice in clearing trades through the Chicago Mercantile Exchange's ClearPort platform or the InterContinentalExchange OTC.

  • NATURAL GAS

    Natural Gas, Basis, LNG and NBP futures and options including: same day options, calendar spread options, financial settlement options, EFS spreads and Pen / Last Day spreads.

  • CRUDE OIL

    WTI and Brent Crude Oil futures and options including: average price options, same day options, calendar spread options, WTI-Brent spread options, financial and physical settlement options.

  • REFINED PRODUCTS

    Heating Oil, RBOB Gasoline, Gasoil, Fuel Oil and Gulf Coast Jet Fuel futures and options including: average price options, same day options, calendar spread options, financial and physical settlement options.
