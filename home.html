Energy Derivatives Brokers

Headquartered in Red Bank, New Jersey, Applegate Commodities LLC brokers natural gas, power, crude oil and refined products derivatives. With over 27 combined years of experience in the industry, brokers Dan Carrion and James Kelly leverage their industry contacts to provide customers with timely information, liquidity and anonymity. Their ability to voice broker and execute trades for customers electronically enables Applegate Commodities to provide competitive bid ask spreads.

Applegate Commodities LLC offers clients the choice in clearing trades through the Chicago Mercantile Exchange's ClearPort platform or the InterContinentalExchange OTC.

Our products

  • Natural Gas

    U.S. Natural Gas Options, Same Day Options, Calendar Spread Options, and Henry Hub Swaps.

  • Power

    Fixed Price, Heat Rates, and Power Options.

  • Crude Oil

    WTI Crude Oil Options, Brent Crude Oil Options, Calendar Spread Options and Same Day Options.

  • Refined Products

    Heating Oil Options, RBOB Gasoline Options, Heat Crack Options, RBOB Crack Options, Calendar Spread Options and GasOil Options.
